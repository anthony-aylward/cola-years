On Feb. 10, 2020, after months of escalating labor action, UC Santa Cruz graduate students began a full wildcat strike in order to secure themselves a cost of living adjustment (COLA). Since then, the UCSC administration has spent $300,000 per day on hiring police to break the strike.

You can help striking grads by donating to their support fund.
